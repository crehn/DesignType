Design Cards Online Quiz

Use our online quiz to learn the arguments of our Design Cards. When ready click the start button. You will get prompted a Design Card description and have to assign it to a dimension e.g. is it a simple or a powerful argument. After choosing the dimension you can choose the name of the Design Card e.g. KISS. For each turn you have 15 seconds. There will be 7 cards to guess. Every right dimension counts one point. For the correct card name you get a second point. Having all cards guessed right grants you one bonus point. When you have finished you may leave your name with your score that may be shown in our highscore section.
