Final Remarks

This categorization is by far not covering all aspects of a developer. Working style, prefered development process, people skills, and many other aspects are neglected here. That's also not the goal of this questionaire. Rather the hope is that this structure helps you to understand how your colleagues think when they make design decisions.

This questionaire is still work in progress. It still hasn't been tried out how well the mapping of the selected statements to design types works. Neither it's clear whether the design types are described correctly. Feedback is welcome.
