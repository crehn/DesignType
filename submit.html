Take Part in the Statistical Analysis

Take part in the statistical analysis by submitting the result. As soon as we have anough statistical data we will present our results here. Our goal is to understand design types better and to correlate design types with experience and working background. Questions we want to answer are:

  • Which are the predominant design types per age, education, and experience level?
  • How does the design type change over time?
  • Which are the predominant design types in certain industry sectors and companies?
  • Which are the predominant design types in certain job positions?
  • ...
